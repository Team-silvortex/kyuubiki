## Why it stays

`tamamono 1.0.0` is meant to move forward with a cleaner product line, but not
by erasing the release evidence that proved the platform was shippable in the
first place.

So this archive stays as:

- the preserved first usable release record
- the historical benchmark for support and smoke breadth
- the handoff point between `0.9.x` release hardening and `1.x` industrial
  hardening
