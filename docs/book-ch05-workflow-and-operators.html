Workflow and operator philosophy

Kyuubiki is moving beyond isolated single operators. The workflow direction is shader-like and neural-network-like composition: operators connected through a stable cross-operator data contract.

Back to book Previous chapter Next chapter

Core workflow ideas

  • Workflow graph growth should be intentional, not ad hoc.
  • Dataset transport should be portable and contract-driven, with an ONNX-like spirit.
  • Operator library ownership remains centralized instead of replicated to every agent.

Why this matters

Once operators can compose reliably, the system can grow toward larger simulation authoring flows without losing headless repeatability or distributed execution discipline.
